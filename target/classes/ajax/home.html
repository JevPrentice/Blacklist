Private Blacklist

'Company' offers a service to docters which enables the features of a large up to date database of patients with outstanding doctors bills, so that YOU the doctor knows whom should and should not be trusted

As a doctor you may blacklist a patient who fails to pay a bill and they will be added to the blacklist, in order to be removed a patient must pay all outstanding debts with any medical practitioner as well as a fee to 'Company'

A 10% fee of outstanding bills is mandatory to be removed from the blacklist
